About Us

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering was established in the year 1998 for B. Tech course. The department offers 4 years of undergraduate program and 2 years of postgraduate program . The department lays stress on all round development of the students ,faculty and laboratory staff by providing them conducive academic environment with necessary infrastructure to carry out their academic and research work. The department has distinguished faculty, most of them holding their Ph.D. degrees.

The Department obtained UGC-Autonomous Status in the year 2014 and since then running the program successfully by meeting all the requirements. The College Academic Council, Board of Studies of the department strive to provide quality education with the most advanced curriculum to make the students industry ready and to excel in the contemporary business world.

The B.Tech Program under Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering was accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Vision

Department is to excel in technical education and research in the area of Electronics and Communication Engineering and to produce skilled, trained and competent individuals with high motivation to meet the present day challenges of the society.

Mission

M1: To impart high quality education to enable students face challenges in the fields of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

M2: To provide facilities, infrastructure, environment to develop the spirit of innovation, creativity, and research among students and faculty.

M3: To inculcate ethical, moral values and lifelong learning skills in students to address the societal needs.

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) Program

The Department was established in the year 1998 with the B. Tech program in Electronics and Communication Engineering. After four years of its inception, in 2002, the department got approval for the M. Tech program from A.I.C.T.E. The department has a team of highly qualified senior faculty with specializations in communication systems, Microwave Engineering, Digital Signal Processing, Digital Image Processing, VLSI and Embedded Systems.

The Objective of the Department is to provide high quality man power by giving the technical inputs to meet the requirements of Indian Industries and R & D establishment both in India and in abroad.

The Department regularly conducts seminars, guest lectures, workshops and technical symposiums on latest technologies. Apart from the curriculum a project development centre is there to impart the real and practical technical skills to the students. The department has been in the fore front for research and training activities in Embedded Systems, Digital Image Processing, Digital Signal Processing, Cryptography and Network Security.

The Department has the best undergraduate program required for the Civil Industry. The course curriculum for the undergraduate program gives the flexibility to the students to prepare for advanced specializations. The department offers a set of electives so that students can plan their academic program in advance. The course structure provides a right mix of compulsory and elective courses.

Curriculum

The B.Tech Program is divided into two distinct parts. A student has to go through a common program called Core Curriculum. Most of the core curriculum is completed in the first four semesters. The last four semesters have a large number of professional courses, that initiate the student to topics in Electronics and Communication Engineering. The Professional Curriculum consists of courses and Project Work. There are not only some compulsory courses but also significant professional electives as well.
