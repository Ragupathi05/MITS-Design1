About Us

The Department offers basic subjects such as English & Foreign Languages, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry,and Humanities. The Department strives to equip students with relevant skills required to excel in this field through an effectively structured and time-tested syllabus.

The Department has obtained UGC-Autonomous Status in the year 2014 and is running the programmes successfully meeting all the requirements. The College Academic Council, Board of Studies of the Department strive to provide quality education and most advanced curriculum and syllabus to make the students industry ready and excel in the contemporary business world.

Personality Development & Communication skills. Coaching for competitive exams like GRE, TOEFL and also GATE.

Vision

“To become a Centre of Excellence in language competencies by empowering global communication aligned with the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages."

Mission

M1: To provide students with a comprehensive education in language and literature, fostering a deep understanding of linguistic and cultural nuances.

M2: To nurture students to become knowledgeable, sensitive, and multilingual individuals and shaping them into global citizens equipped to thrive in an interconnected world.

M3: To empower students to engage actively and thoughtfully in diverse social contexts, from local to global perspectives, promoting informed participation and cultural sensitivity.
